The Real Estate Academy
Curriculum Master Plan
Version 1.0

Purpose
The Curriculum Master Plan is the master blueprint for the Academy. It defines the complete learning journey from orientation through graduation. Every future lesson, case study, AI Lab, assessment, and capstone project originates from this document.

Curriculum Philosophy
The Academy develops complete professionals—not merely licensed Realtors. Character, knowledge, business, technology, leadership, and lifelong learning are developed together.

Assessment Strategy
Each module concludes with quizzes, scenario-based exercises, and an Academy Project demonstrating real-world competence.

Curriculum Design Questions
Every lesson should answer:
• Why does this matter?
• How is it applied in Ontario?
• How would an exceptional Realtor use it?
• How can technology improve it?
• How does it connect to the Academy?

Vision
The Curriculum Master Plan is a living document that evolves with legislation, technology, markets, and professional best practices while remaining rooted in the Founding Papers.
